Bloor and Dufferin Development

Bloor and Dufferin Development

Contract Value: ~$10M

Project Scope

  • Earthworks
  • Underground Servicing
  • Roadworks and Sidewalks

Work Scope

  • Full time inspection on site
  • Ensure IFC drawing & City of Toronto construction specs are followed
  • Reporting construction schedule with city staff
  • Coordination of water turn on/off and all required testings

Earthworks

  • Comprehensive site preparation involving grading, excavation, and fill placement
  • Coordination with soil engineer of imported backfill material (native soil not suitable)
  • Major issue identified during proof roll
  • Large quantity sub excavates and re work coordination

Underground Servicing

  • Installation of watermain, sewer and all relevant utilities
  • Coordination with utility providers (gas and hydro)
  • Adherence to all safety and regulatory standards
  • Maintain good communication & relationship with city staff
  • Work with GC and Sub, conduct all required testing
  • Drafted and submitted Toronto Water Wellness Report

Roadworks

  • Construction of a durable road surface designed for heavy traffic loads
  • Implementation of advanced paving techniques
  • Incorporation of traffic calming measures
